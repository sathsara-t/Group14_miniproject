Your Academic Edge

The Mastery Optimizer combines two of the most powerful mental models for success: The 10,000-Hour Rule and The 80/20 Pareto Principle. Instead of studying harder, you will study strategically—focusing on the vital few subjects that yield the highest GPA impact while tracking your path to true mastery.

⚡ The 80/20 Pareto Principle

Invented by Vilfredo Pareto, this principle suggests that 80% of your outcomes come from 20% of your causes. By analyzing your module grades, our system identifies the exact 20% of your curriculum that is dragging down your overall GPA. By allocating 60% of your study time strictly to these weak points, you can achieve exponential growth in your academic performance.

🔥 The 10,000-Hour Rule

Popularized by Malcolm Gladwell, the rule states that world-class expertise requires 10,000 hours of deliberate practice. Our tracker weights deliberate, focused practice more heavily than passive reading. This tool lets you log every session, visibly progressing toward the mastery milestone in your core subjects.
